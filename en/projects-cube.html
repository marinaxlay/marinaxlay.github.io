Context and objectives

Introduction

Its aim is to identify and synthesize all information necessary for all CFT employees regardless of their expertise, and centralize it to facilitate information searching.

Several UX projects are conducted each year on CUBE to ensure continuous improvement. During one of these previous projects, users spontaneously gave us the following feedback about the homepage:

  • "I find the homepage really empty. The white background with text on it doesn't attract me, it doesn't speak to me."

A redesign of the homepage was therefore deemed necessary, with several key challenges:

Project challenges :

    Other UX projects on this product

    Redesigning wiki page templates

    Objective: Improve CUBE's visual identity, aiming for clarity and sobriety to encourage adoption and enhance the experience of the platform for all.‚Äù

    Methods: 3 rounds of interviews (with 31 users) :

    1. Ideation: Open-ended questions, collection of their impressions of the existing wiki pages, production of low-fi wireframes.
    2. Suggestion: Presentation of wireframes to users, solution-finding process, production of medium/high-fidelity prototypes.
    3. Test: Presentation of prototype to users, validation of solutions by users.

    Solutions:

    • Integration of tabs within pages to reduce scrolling and lighten users' cognitive load by guaranteeing better information architecture,
    • Revision of clickable text actions to limit text elements, better differentiate them from body text and lighten users' cognitive load,
    • Redefinition of title styles to ensure better readability of information and take a step towards the Group charter.

    Redesign of the portal's tree structure

    Objective: ‚ÄúBetter guide users through the portal with clear navigation by reorganizing the entries in the navigation bar and offering alternative navigation methods.‚Äù

    Methods:

    1. Card sorting workshop: Get users to cooperate on page categorization.
    2. Tree testing workshop: Validate or challenge the new tree structure.

    Solutions:

    • A navigation bar limited to broad themes rather than an exhaustive list of existing pages,
    • Clearer headings, enabling more effective categorization of subjects, and thus a more intuitive search for information on the portal.

    Redesign of the document publication journey @ contributors

    Objective: "Simplify document publication by offering a more intuitive user journey."

    How?:

    • By lightening the 'add a document' form,
    • By modernizing the error or confirmation pop-up for adding a document.

    Methods: 2 phases de tests (avec 14 utilisateurs) :

    1. User tests: Presentation of wireframe, adjustment of wireframes based on feedback.
    2. User tests: Presentation of prototype, polishing of prototypes based on feedback.

    Feedbacks:

    • "It's much better to have just one field for title and name."
    • "It's great to have all the information you need in case you make a mistake."

    Redesign of the document display component @ visitors

    Objective: "Simplify access to documents by offering a more intuitive display mode."

    How?:

    • By gathering all the documents on one page without distinction of language,
    • By specifying the language on the document,
    • By identifying a default sort and adding a filter system (by language, by audience, by alphabetical order...).

    Methods: 2 phases de tests (avec 14 utilisateurs) :

    1. User tests: Presentation of wireframe, adjustment of wireframes based on feedback.
    2. User tests: Presentation of prototype, polishing of prototypes based on feedback.

    Feedbacks:

    • "It's great. More pleasant and intuitive."
    • "There are fewer buttons, it's less heavy, it's even better, it's perfect."
