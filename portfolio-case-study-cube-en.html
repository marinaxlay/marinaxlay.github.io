CUBE

Designing a showcase homepage for one of Soci√©t√© G√©n√©rale's IT directions.

Time frame: February - July 2023

Tools: Figma

Team: Boris Durupt-Zhou, Camille Kui

Device : Desktop

In a nutshell :

CUBE suffered from a home page that users found uninviting and unintuitive. Through a comprehensive UX approach, we rethought its design to enhance its attractiveness and effectiveness. This project illustrates the importance of a user-centered approach in transforming a knowledge management portal into a truly effective internal communication tool.

  • Problems identified : A cold, static interface, lack of dynamism and clarity (of information and target audience).
  • Skills applied : User interviews, Workshop, Wireframing, Prototyping, User testing, Iterative design.
  • Solutions : Modernization of the design, implementation of the Group's Design System, integration of dynamic content and optimized information architecture.
  • Results : A more intuitive interface, more engaging and better adapted to user needs, validated by tests and demonstrated through a satisfaction survey.

CUBE is the knowledge management portal for CFT (Corporate Functions Technologies), one of Soci√©t√© G√©n√©rale's IT departments. Its aim is to identify and synthesize all information necessary for all CFT employees regardless of their expertise, and centralize it to facilitate information searching.

Several UX projects are conducted each year on CUBE to ensure continuous improvement. During one of these previous projects, users spontaneously gave us the following feedback about the homepage:

üí≠ "I find the homepage really empty. The white background with text on it doesn't attract me, it doesn't speak to me."

üí≠ "My first impression is that it's rather cold. It lacks dynamic elements."

üí≠ "I wonder if there's a way to do something visually. It feels like it's the emptiest and simplest page of the entire site."

üí≠ "When I land on the homepage, it looks dead."

A redesign of the homepage was therefore deemed necessary, with several key challenges:

Project challenges :

  • Give visitors a better first impression of CUBE.
  • Improve the attractiveness of CFT.
  • Strengthen CUBE's positioning as an internal showcase for CFT.
