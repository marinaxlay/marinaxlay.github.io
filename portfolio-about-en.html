Hi! I'm Marina, a UX/UI designer based in Lille, France. With a background in illustration and technical communication, I blend creativity with ergonomy to craft designs that are not just beautiful but also functional and most of all, user-centric.

And when I say user, I mean all users. My design philosophy is simple: great design should feel effortless, and that's why I strongly believe accessibility in design is crucial to driving better digital experiences for everyone regardless of ability.

I'm always eager to improve and learn new skills, and always on the lookout for new tools that will help me refine my workflow.

When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me either playing cozy videogames, painting or crocheting over a cup of tea, or crowdsurfing at a metal show.
